About Let's Eat

Here is what we are about:

Let's Eat was created by Alastair Nicdao in the year 2012 in Layton, Utah. It first started off by him just loving all types of foods. His idea was why not show people around the world the art and love for food. We all know all of us humans have such a desire for foods, so he was very passionate to share it with the whole world. So as time went by he followed the nine steps. Which has helped him to be successful as the years went by. After getting connected with a buisness partner, Giada De Laurentiis, he then started pitching his ideas to her, which began to be a good successful side project for them. As they got more and more people he then wanted to expand the buisness to a city that's all about food and has an amazing nightlife. So he decided to move Let's Eat to Los Angeles. It started off as just hiring people across the world for the website then as he got more and more chefs on his website him and his partner have decided to open up a restaurant in Los Angeles. It will be up and running in summer 2022. More Information
